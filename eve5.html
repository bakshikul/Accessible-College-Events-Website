GSoC Orientation and Preparation Talk

May 8, 2026


Overview


This session provides a complete overview of Google Summer of Code and how students can prepare effectively to increase their chances of selection.


Topics Covered


  • What is Google Summer of Code.
  • Understanding timelines and milestones.
  • Writing a strong proposal.
  • Communicating with mentors.
  • Choosing the right organization.

Who Should Attend


  • Open-source contributors.
  • Students interested in GSoC.
  • Beginners seeking mentorship programs.

Outcome


Attendees will gain a clear roadmap for GSoC preparation and understand how to approach the application process strategically.


Registration


Registration is open to all students. Seats are limited and will be allotted on a first-come, first-served basis.
