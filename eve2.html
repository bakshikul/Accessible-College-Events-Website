Open Source Contribution Meetup

March 27, 2026


Overview


This meetup is designed to help students understand how open-source communities work and how to make their first successful contribution.


Topics Covered


  • What is open source and why it matters.
  • Finding beginner-friendly issues.
  • Understanding GitHub workflows.
  • Writing effective commit messages.
  • Communicating with maintainers .

Who Should Attend


  • Students new to open source.
  • Developers interested in collaborationt.
  • Anyone preparing for GSoC or similar programs.

Outcome


Attendees will learn the complete process of contributing to open-source projects and gain confidence to submit their first pull request.


Registration


Registration is open to all students. Seats are limited and will be allotted on a first-come, first-served basis.
