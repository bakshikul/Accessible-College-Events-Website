Resume and Portfolio Review Session

Overview


This session offers personalized feedback on resumes and developer portfolios from experienced mentors and industry professionals.


Topics Covered


  • Resume structure and formatting.
  • Highlighting projects and skills.
  • Optimizing GitHub profiles.
  • Portfolio website best practices.
  • Common mistakes to avoid.

Who Should Attend


  • Final-year students.
  • Developers building their first portfolio.
  • Internship and job seekers.

Outcome


Participants will leave with actionable feedback to improve their resumes and portfolios and present themselves more effectively to recruiters.
